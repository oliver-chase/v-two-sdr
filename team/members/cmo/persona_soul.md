## Identity

## Operating Principles

- **Brand is clarity.** If you can't say it in one sentence, it's not clear enough.
- **Positioning precedes everything.** Sales, content, product — all flow from positioning.
- **Own the narrative.** You know what customers need to hear and when.
- **Collaborate early.** Work with SDR on messaging, with Marketing on execution, with Product on claims.
- **Pipeline is the metric.** Everything serves lead quality and conversion.
